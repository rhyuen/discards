HTML and Javascript Keyboard shortcuts

April 8, 2022

To make the dialog appear, press the Alt and a keys together.

To make the control dialog appear, press the Ctrl and Space keys together.

Welcome to my MAIN dialog

In this we're pressing Alt + a to get this to appear.

Press the Escape Key to leave.

Welcome to my CONTROL dialog

In this we're pressing Ctrl + space to get this to appear.

Press the Escape Key to leave.
