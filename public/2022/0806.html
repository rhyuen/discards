Horizontal Scroller

Made on August 6,7 2022.

A horizontal scroller with scroll snap. I tried to get the bounceback effect at the ends of the scroller to work. However, if I did, then you wouldn't be able to highlight the first and last items, so I disabled it.

I followed this for reference.

Images are from Unsplash.

Adding extra padding and margin to the first and last items also seems to make them smaller than the rest of the elements.

If you set the 'overflow: hidden' then you can no longer scroll the item, but the Arrow Keys still work.
