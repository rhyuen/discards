Whalies

When whales surface form the ocean, they release phytoplankton which are responsible for 80% of the world's oxygen. They're also crucial for absorbing green house gases and thus crucial in the fight to curb emissions. Unfortunately, as of late, whales have been washing up ashore on beaches dead filled with large quantities of plastic. They're also being hunted for food in Japan.
